Download your Life Calendar!

On this page, you can download a copy of your life calendar, with a preview supported and ready-to-print This downloaded copy is high-quality, and you can use it to either make your own or simply hang it on a wall (since it syncs with your account if you are a registered user).

You can customize your personal life calendar below. There are two main options - granularity and calendar type.

Granularity dictates the life calendar size (i.e., whether to have a life calendar in weeks, in months, etc.).

Calendar Type affects the life calendar type - blank or full. If you do not select blank, you will have a life calendar available as an image to you that mirrors your current life calendar (in colors and position). If you select blank, you will simply receive a blank life calendar that pertains to your age expectancy.
