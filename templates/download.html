Download your Life Calendar!

On this page, you can download a copy of your life calendar, in a few formats. This downloaded copy is high-quality, and you can use it to either make your own or simply hang it on a wall (since it syncs with your account if you are a registered user).

It supports both blank images and images like the one on the homepage. You can select the granularity as well (from weeks to years - shorter/longer is unfeasible).
